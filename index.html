The Littlest Pet Shop is a toy franchise line owned by Hasbro. The first Littlest Pet Shops were made in 90’s, when the brand was owned by Keller and the toys looked more like realistic miniature animal figurines.

When the franchise relaunched in 2005, the producers decided to mix the modern with the traditional. They wanted to reach a broader audience and ditched the pink packaging. The redesign gave the animals big bobble heads, big vulnerable eyes and a more realistic color palette. The brand was a super hit and Littlest Pet Shop went from a 60 million dollar brand to 600 million dollar in just 5 years, making it one of the biggest toy brands in the world.
